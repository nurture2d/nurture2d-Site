Made for your workflow

Designed with animators in mind. Clean interface, intuitive tools, and seamless integration with your creative process.

Made for you

Built by animators, for animators. Every feature crafted to enhance your creative vision and streamline your production.

Transparent gap filling

Fill transparent pixel gaps using lasso and rectangle selection tools.

Auto Color Line fill tools

This feature fills non-antialised preset lines automatically. Useful for cell shading to achieve that anime look.

Custom sketch brush engine

Natural pencil textures, pressure curves, and jitter for expressive sketches.

Precise cleanup tools

Efficient erasing, smoothing, and line refinement for clean, production-ready frames.

Export with confidence

Render to image sequences neatly exported in separate folders, ready for production and post-processing.
